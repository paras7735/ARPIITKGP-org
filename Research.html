Research
Department of Architecture and Regional Planning is widely acclaimed for the quality and breadth of its research enterprise, and particularly for its openness to multidisciplinary research. In this continuous quest for excellence in our research endeavours, we recognize the importance of modernization in infrastructure and experimental facilities. To ensure that we hold on to our position at the cutting edge of innovation, the Institute has set up several state of the art facilities.
